About me

I am a PhD student at the Pennsylvania State University, advised by Prof. Minghui Zhu.
I received the B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2019.

In my research, I am always thinking about how to develop an agent with a generalist policy or one that can adapt its policy model to multi-task with multi-environment. My recent research interests mainly focus on reinforcement learning, optimization, and robot learning.
