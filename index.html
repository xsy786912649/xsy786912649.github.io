About me

I am a fourth year PhD student at the Pennsylvania State University, advised by Prof. Minghui Zhu.
I received the B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2019.

My research interests mainly focus on optimization, learning theory, and robot learning.
